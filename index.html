Explore the Solar System ...

The solar system is a collection of celestial objects bound together by gravity, with the Sun at its center. It includes planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and other space objects.

How It All Works Together. - The solar system is structured with the Sun at the center, followed by the inner planets, asteroid belt, outer planets, and then the Kuiper Belt and Oort Cloud at the farthest reaches. The gravitational pull of the Sun governs the orbits of all these objects, creating a dynamic system in constant motion.
The solar system is just a tiny part of our galaxy, the Milky Way, which contains billions of other stars and planetary systems. Studying the solar system helps us understand our place in the universe and the forces that shape planetary formation and life.
